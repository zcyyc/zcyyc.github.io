Chenyu Zhao · Ph.D. Researcher · Nankai University

AI for Reliable Systems

Building intelligent, observable, and resilient systems for real-world operations.

I am a Ph.D. student in the College of Software at Nankai University, working at the intersection of artificial intelligence and reliable software systems. Before beginning my doctoral study, I spent two years as an R&D engineer at Alibaba Cloud Intelligence Group, gaining first-hand experience with the reliability challenges of large-scale production systems.

My research focuses on AI for Reliable Systems, particularly agentic failure diagnosis and recovery, reliable software engineering agents, AI system evaluation, and intelligent operations for cloud and distributed systems. I am interested not only in developing new AI techniques, but also in turning them into dependable solutions for real-world operational environments.

My long-term goal is to bridge academic research and production practice, and to extend reliable intelligent technologies to critical infrastructure such as cloud computing, communications, energy, and transportation.
